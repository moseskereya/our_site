CLIMBING MOUNT KILIMANJARO

We offers an exceptional product for small groups or individuals wanting to climb Kilimanjaro. This package includes transport and accomodation before and after the ascent to Kilimanjaro.

We have extensive expertise in Kilimanjaro and is a partner of IMEC and KPAP, two organizations that ensure exceptional service. All our climbs are supervised by KPAP (kilimanjaro Poters Association Project). East African Our company is one of the best companies that provide fair treatment for porters.

All our climbs are guaranteed, so a registration is enough to guarantee the climb. The maximum number of people in each group is 12 people. It is therefore small groups facilitating the customization of the service. If desired, the group can be provided with a French speaking guide in the ascent, at no additional cost.

Climb Mt.Kilimanjaro in 6 days – Machame route (+1 free day in Arusha)

This is an open climb to the peak of Africa via the Machame route. Machame route is one of the wild and most preserved routes to the roof of Africa hence it is more challenging in terms of terrain and also walking time. This ascent is the most challenging climb we offer and it is recommended for the physically confident individuals as it is quite physically challenging. On this tour, you will ascend the mountain and descend via different routes hence there will be a wide variety of landscape and vegetation environment throughout the 6 days.

Climb Mt. Kilimanjaro in 7 days – Machame route (+1 free day in Arusha)

This is a 7-day group mountaineering to the summit of Kilimanjaro. The Machame route, also known as “the Whiskey Way”, is one of the most beautiful routes to climb Kilimanjaro, especially because of the diversity of its landscape. While on the Machame route, you will journey through thick forests, groundsel trees, volcanic canyons and on the last days through seracs and ice glaciers. Enjoy the view along the way.

Climb Mt. Kilimanjaro in 8 days – Lemosho route (+2 Days in Arusha)

Discover the magical landscape during your ascent to Kilimanjaro on the Lemosho route. The spectacular hike through the Shira Plateau is what makes this route truly special. The Lemosho route is the least traveled but most scenic route towards the peak of Kilimanjaro. An ascent via the Lemosho route takes longer than through the Machame route because climbers need to be better acclimatization in this route and, thus, have a better chance to reach the summit!

Add a group safari to your Kilimanjaro climb

Eager to explore more of Tanzania’s wonders? Add a 3-day group safari to your Kilimanjaro ascent. This is an opportunity to discover 3 parks in 3 days: Ngorongoro National Park, Tarangire National Park and Lake Manyara National Park.
